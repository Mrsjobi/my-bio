Chocolate Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 250 gm all purpose flour
  • 4 beaten egg
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 250 gm powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoon cocoa powder
  • 1 cup butter

Instructions

  1. To prepare this easy chocolate cake recipe, pre-heat the oven to 180Â°C.
  2. Grease and line a 7-inch round cake tin with baking paper and butter.
  3. Sieve together the flour, cocoa powder and baking powder. Keep the dry ingredients aside.
  4. Take a glass bowl and mix butter and sugar into it. Beat these ingredients till light and fluffy
  5. Beat in the eggs, two at a time and allowing at least two minutes gap between each addition
  6. Lightly fold in the flour into the mixture. Pour the batter into the prepared tin and bake for 35 to 40 minutes.
  7. Check if the cake is baked properly by inserting a toothpick into the centre. If it comes out clean, then the cake is done.
